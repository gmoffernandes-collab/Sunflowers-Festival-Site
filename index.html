About the Festival

Sunflowers Festival 2026 is a sustainable music festival inspired by the freedom, music and positive atmosphere of the 1970s.


The festival combines live music, renewable energy, eco-friendly activities and nature to create an unforgettable experience.

Sustainability Plan

☀ Solar Energy

Solar panels will provide clean energy for the festival.

♻ Recycling

Recycling stations will be available all around the venue.

🥤 Reusable Cups

Reusable cups will reduce plastic waste during the event.

🌱 Local Food

Local food trucks will support sustainable businesses.
